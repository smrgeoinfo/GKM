La Tojiza Plutonni back to ToC or Named Individual ToC

IRI: http://loop3d.org/GSO/ontology/2020/1/ex-plutontojiza#latojizapluton

medium- to coarse-grained granite, consists of quartz, K-feldspar, plagioclase and biotite. Apatite, ilmenite, zircon, muscovite and, locally, garnet are accessory minerals. The amount of muscovite and garnet increases, and grain size decreases, towards the eastern and southern pluton borders. but most samples are equigranular. Both textural types contain rounded grains of quartz visible to the naked eye, pointing to the lack of solid-state deformation. Large xenoliths and roofpendants of country rocks occur mainly in the central part of the intrusion and enclaves of basic igneous rocks in its eastern part.

pluton c
