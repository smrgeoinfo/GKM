Laramide Orogeny ni back to ToC or Named Individual ToC

IRI: http://loop3d.org/GSO/ontology/2020/1/ex-history#Laramide_Orogeny

Late Cretaceous to Early Tertiary Deformation event in western North America, generally considered to have occurred between 80 Ma and 40 Ma. Manifestations vary in different parts of the western North American Cordillera, but eastward migration of magmatism and deformation, particularly in the western USA are characteristic.
